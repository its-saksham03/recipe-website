Fudgy Chocolate Brownie

Vegetarian Dessert Medium
15
Prep (min)
25
Bake (min)
40
Total (min)
12
Pieces

Deeply chocolatey, with a shiny crackly top and a fudgy gooey centre. Made with real dark chocolate, these brownies will ruin you for box brownies forever.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate (70%), chopped
  • 150g unsalted butter, cubed
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 100g all-purpose flour
  • 30g cocoa powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 100g chocolate chips or walnuts (optional)

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat and prep. Preheat oven to 180°C (160°C fan). Grease and line a 20×20 cm baking tin with parchment paper.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter. In a heatproof bowl over simmering water, melt chopped chocolate and butter together, stirring until glossy and smooth. Cool slightly.
  3. Whisk eggs and sugar. Vigorously whisk eggs, sugar, and vanilla for 3–4 minutes until pale, thick, and doubled in volume. This creates the crackly top.
  4. Fold in chocolate. Pour the cooled chocolate into the egg mixture. Gently fold together until just combined.
  5. Add dry ingredients. Sift in flour, cocoa, and salt. Fold gently until no flour streaks remain. Fold in chocolate chips or walnuts if using.
  6. Bake. Pour into tin and spread evenly. Bake 22–25 minutes. A toothpick should come out with moist crumbs. The centre will look slightly underdone — that's correct!
  7. Cool completely. Let cool in tin for at least 1 hour before cutting. For extra clean slices, refrigerate 30 min first.
