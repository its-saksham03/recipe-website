Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes

Vegetarian Easy Breakfast
5
Prep (min)
15
Cook (min)
20
Total (min)
8
Pancakes

These cloud-like buttermilk pancakes are incredibly tall and fluffy. The secret? Don't overmix the batter — lumps are your friend! Serve stacked high with maple syrup and fresh berries.

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1¼ cups buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter for greasing pan
  • Maple syrup and berries, to serve

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the centre.
  2. Mix wet ingredients. In a jug, whisk buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine. Pour wet into dry. Stir gently until JUST combined. Lumps are fine! Overmixing = flat pancakes.
  4. Rest the batter. Let batter rest 5 minutes. This activates the baking powder for extra fluffiness.
  5. Cook the pancakes. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low. Butter it lightly. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form and edges look set (2–3 min), then flip and cook 1–2 min more.
  6. Serve. Keep cooked pancakes in a warm oven (90°C) until all done. Serve stacked with maple syrup and fresh berries.
